The "Ganzfeld" experiment remains the most cited evidence in parapsychological literature for "anomalous information transfer" (psi).
*   **The Study:** A 2023 meta-analysis published in the *Journal of Anomalous Experience and Cognition* examined decades of Ganzfeld data. *   **Mainstream Scientific Consensus:** The broader scientific community remains highly skeptical. ### 2. Research on Precognition (The "Bem" Legacy)
